Project EDR

Also known by the team as "Aidentifye". An Eye Disease Recognition Web App that takes in an eye picture and outputs if the eye is normal or has a disease. Using CNN, we trained a model on publicly available eye images in the web which resulted in a limited but proof-of-concept application. It was intended to be a potential assistant to eye experts that can crosscheck the results of the app. But if it was given even more quality data and training experimentation, it can serve non-experts too in doing initial checks even if limited. Definitely not intended to replace eye experts but it is a neat proof-of-concept or prototype.

Features

  • - Takes an eye image through device camera

  • - Outputs a label based on if the eye photo suggested a normal eye or an eye with a disease, limited to Trachoma, etc. etc. etc.
